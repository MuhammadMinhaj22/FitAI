About FitAI

How It Works

Our AI Fitness & Diet Planner takes your personal data – age, weight, height, gender, fitness goal, and activity level – to create a customized fitness and nutrition plan just for you. The advanced algorithm calculates your BMI, and based on your goals, generates a comprehensive 7-day workout and diet schedule.

Benefits of a Healthy Lifestyle

A healthy lifestyle is not just about the numbers on a scale. It's about feeling good, having more energy, improving your mood, and reducing your risk of chronic diseases. Regular physical activity and a balanced diet are the cornerstones of a healthy life, leading to better physical and mental well-being.

Our Philosophy

We believe that fitness should be accessible to everyone. Our goal is to provide a simple, effective, and free tool to help you on your fitness journey. We focus on home-based workouts and simple, nutritious meal plans that you can easily integrate into your daily life.
